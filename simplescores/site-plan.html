Overview

Purpose

I want to create a film music database that allows people to find and purchase the music they want from various websites. As film music is often released in different collections, some more complete than others, my plan for this website is to list all official soundtrack releases for a specific film and make it easy to purchase them by linking them to broader websites like Amazon and Walmart or more niche sites like La La Land Records, a record company that releases expanded special editions of film scores to the public in special box sets.

Audience

My audience is people who care about film music and want to enjoy them in the most complete way currently possible

WHY THIS WEBSITE EXISTS The world of film music is complicated and aggravating to people invested in film scores. After a film is composed, the film studio retains the rights to the score, and this can lead to film scores losing the attention and respect they deserve. Film studios will often release an ‚Äúoriginal soundtrack‚Äù at the release of a film, however these releases are often incomplete, with major cues (segments of music) cut out entirely or saved as ‚Äúbonus tracks‚Äù for buying the soundtrack at certain retailers or as a digital album. Thankfully, film studios will often release expanded or complete scores down the line in special editions, released at occasions like anniversaries or sequel releases. However, these are often limited in quantity and/or available dates, released by third-party record labels outside of mainstream retailers, or poorly advertised, leading to confusion for the average family member or friend wanting to purchase a gift for their music loving loved one. This website aims to help fix that. HOW IT WORKS The goal of this website is to present any given film‚Äôs released score in a way that allows fans of film music to find the releases they want. Whether that be options to purchase them from retailers providing them, or the information needed to find out-of-print releases second-hand, this website aims to provide those resources. Each film will have its own page where each release‚Äôs information will be listed along with links (if available) to retailers to purchase them. If a release is out-of-print, links to second-hand markets such as eBay, OfferUp, and Craigslist will be provided where those releases might be able to be found.
