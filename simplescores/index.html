WELCOME TO SIMPLESCORES

The world of film music is complicated and aggravating to people invested in film scores. After a film is composed, the film studio retains the rights to the score, and this can lead to film scores losing the attention and respect they deserve. Film studios will often release an “original soundtrack” at the release of a film, however these releases are often incomplete, with major cues (segments of music) cut out entirely or saved as “bonus tracks” for buying the soundtrack at certain retailers or as a digital album. Thankfully, film studios will often release expanded or complete scores down the line in special editions, released at occasions like anniversaries or sequel releases. However, these are often limited in quantity and/or available dates, released by third-party record labels outside of mainstream retailers, or poorly advertised, leading to confusion for the average family member or friend wanting to purchase a gift for their music loving loved one. This website aims to help fix that.

HOW IT WORKS

The goal of this website is to present any given film’s released score in a way that allows fans of film music to find the releases they want. Whether that be options to purchase them from retailers providing them, or the information needed to find out-of-print releases second-hand, this website aims to provide those resources.

Each film will have its own page where each release’s information will be listed along with links (if available) to retailers to purchase them. If a release is out-of-print, links to second-hand markets such as eBay, OfferUp, and Craigslist will be provided where those releases might be able to be found.
